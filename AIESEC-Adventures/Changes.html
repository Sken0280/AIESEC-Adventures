The main issue that caused a drastic change in the initial design of the website is a result of a couple reasons. The first reason is that out of the 20 people i reached out to asking if they wanted to share their story, only 1 person was actually comfortable sharing which was really really un-forseen. Katherine was the only person that gave a detailed overview of her exchange. The others that went to taiwan this past summer didn't give detailed testimonials and lacked sufficient pictures. The second reason that caused the shift of the website's contents deals with the interests of prospective exchange participants. During this semester AIESEC Laurier conducted 28 consultations with individuals who were gung-ho about going on exchange. 26/28 of these individuals were mainly looking for opportunities in Taiwan, and 2 of the other individuals decided they no longer wanted to go on exchange. This huge focus towards taiwan was another reason why we decided to go ahead with using the 2 testimonials we had for Taiwan.
